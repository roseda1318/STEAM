Curriculum.

Why should k-8 have all the fun? The goal of this curriculum is to bring fun flashy tech projects to all 9-12 computer science student.

Ideally, the students completing this curriculum will have complete a computer science course before. For the NHS students, those courses are Intro to CS, AP-Computer Science A, or AP-Computer Science Principles. This curriculum is a half of year and will be a mixture of guided projects and independent studies.

This curriculum has a focus on Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math concepts, they all require a coding and a design element. There will be several access points that allow customization for students at all programming levels. Students will have the option to explore Java, Python, Unity, and JavaScript languages.
